Shotmap Collection

A collection of shotmaps build using publicly available shots data, python, and MPLSoccer.

Marcus Thuram's shotmaps from the last three seasons (the 2024/25 season was taken a couple of months in), in his first season with Inter Milan (middle map) he scored a number of goals from the six-yard box.

Here we have three of Europe's top strikers, Alexander Isak, Moise Kean, and Robert Lewandowski that tend to feast from opportunities in the central area close to goal. They are lethal from inside the six-yard box. The exception being that one small red dot in Kean's shot map, a goal from range against Inter Milan, Kean rolls the ball for a simple finish after Yann Sommer rushes out from his goal.

Last but not least, here we have three strikers with different domains. Real Madrid's Kylian Mbappé tends to drift from the left channel, with Mo Salah favoring the opposite side for Liverpool. Atalanta's Mateo Retegui on the other hand, tends to stay central.
